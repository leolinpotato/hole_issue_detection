Overall Image Realistic Score

1 (Not Realistic): The image has severe flaws and is easily identifiable as non-realistic.
2 (Barely Realistic): The image has multiple flaws but could pass as a low-quality or stylized representation of reality.
3 (Somewhat Realistic): The image is plausible at a glance, with some aspects that may not hold up to scrutiny.
4 (Very Realistic): The image is very convincing, with any flaws being minor and requiring close inspection to identify.
5 (Indistinguishable from Reality): The image is virtually indistinguishable from a high-quality photograph; any flaws are imperceptible.

Overall Image Aesthetic Score

1 (Poor Aesthetics): The aesthetic elements are severely flawed and do not contribute positively to the overall appeal. The image lacks any discernible aesthetic qualities.
2 (Below Average Aesthetics): The image has multiple aesthetic flaws and falls short of creating a visually pleasing or engaging experience. Aesthetic elements are present but are poorly executed.
3 (Average Aesthetics): The image is aesthetically acceptable, with some pleasing elements, but overall, it lacks a strong visual impact. There is room for improvement in the arrangement and execution of aesthetic features.
4 (Good Aesthetics): The image displays well-executed aesthetic elements that contribute positively to the overall visual appeal. A viewer would find the image visually pleasing, with minor areas for enhancement.
5 (Excellent Aesthetics): The image is exceptional in its aesthetic qualities, demonstrating a high level of creativity, skill, and attention to detail. A viewer would find the image visually captivating, with virtually no room for improvement.
